About Us

             Welcome to Antagonist Festival, where music meets self-discovery in a unique and unapologetic celebration of the human experience. Antagonist is not just a festival; it's an immersive journey into the depths of our psyche, where pop, hyperpop, trap, R&B, and techno provide the soundtrack to your personal transformation.
             This is a judgment-free zone. Antagonist invites you to shed the societal expectations and be the villain if you wish. Let the music pulse through you, allowing the beats to unearth aspects of yourself that you may not have acknowledged before. It's a cathartic experience where you can confront your inner antagonist, embracing the complexities that make you human. But here's the twist - as the music reverberates through the festival grounds, we encourage you to leave these shadows behind within the gates of Antagonist. In this space, you have the freedom to explore the depths of your being, knowing that you won't be judged for being the antagonist in your own story. Antagonist is not just about indulgence; it's about transformation. As you dance to the beats that mirror the rhythm of your own heart, let this festival be a catalyst for change. Leave the bad qualities, the inner villains, behind and emerge from Antagonist as the hero of your own narrative.

             Join us at Antagonist, where the music, the experience, and the community come together to redefine what it means to be human. Embrace the antagonist within, and let the festival be your stage for self-discovery and growth. After all, at Antagonist, we believe that in confronting our shadows, we find the light that makes us truly heroic.

FAQ

1. Can I upgrade my ticket after purchase?
Yes, you can upgrade your ticket any time after purchase, but no later than 2 weeks before the festival. However, you cannot downgrade.

2. Can I bring medicine inside the festival?
Bringing medicine on the festival grounds is only allowed along with a prescription from your doctor. You shouldn't bring more than what was prescribed, otherwise our security will seize any extra amount. You must keep a copy of your prescription with you at all times during the festival to avoid unnecessary controls.

3. Can I delete or edit my check-in?
Yes, but no later than 1 month after purchase. Otherwise, will you have to check-in at the gate and pay the 25$ fee.

4. Can I bring my pet with me?
We would not recommend exposing your furry friend to such loud music and big crowds. It might make them nervous, scared or agitated. The decision of bringing your pet is yours, don't forget their leash and a GPS collar so they don'y get lost.
